## Preface / 前言

### English

This textbook presents the first comprehensive treatment of AI Neuroscience (AINEURO), a pioneering interdisciplinary field that studies artificial intelligence systems through the lens of neuroscience. As AI systems become increasingly complex and capable, the need for systematic scientific understanding of their internal mechanisms becomes paramount. This book bridges the gap between computer science and neuroscience, offering a unified framework for understanding, analyzing, and designing intelligent systems.

The book is organized into three major parts corresponding to the three theoretical pillars of AINEURO:
1. **Silicon-Based Neuroarchitecture** - Understanding hardware as neural systems
2. **Algorithmic Cognitive Mapping** - Connecting AI algorithms to brain functions  
3. **Phenomenology of Consciousness** - Exploring the possibility of machine consciousness

Each chapter includes theoretical foundations, practical applications, and thought-provoking exercises. The bilingual format (English primary, Chinese secondary) makes this book accessible to a global audience while preserving the nuances of both Western and Eastern scientific traditions.

### 中文

本教科书首次全面介绍了人工智能神经科学（AINEURO），这是一门通过神经科学视角研究人工智能系统的开创性交叉学科。随着AI系统变得越来越复杂和强大，对其内部机制进行系统性科学理解的需求变得至关重要。本书架起了计算机科学与神经科学之间的桥梁，为理解、分析和设计智能系统提供了统一的框架。

本书按照AINEURO的三大理论支柱组织成三个主要部分：
1. **硅基神经架构** - 将硬件理解为神经系统
2. **算法认知映射** - 将AI算法与大脑功能相连接
3. **意识现象学** - 探索机器意识的可能性

每章都包含理论基础、实际应用和发人深省的练习。双语格式（英文为主，中文为辅）使本书能够被全球读者阅读，同时保留了西方和东方科学传统的精髓。
