# IEEE TSBI Cover Design / 期刊封面设计

## Volume 1, Number 1 | February 2026

### Cover Art Description / 封面艺术描述

The cover design for IEEE Transactions on Silicon-Based Intelligence Volume 1 Number 1 follows the professional engineering standards of IEEE publications:

**Visual Elements:**
- **Header Bar**: IEEE blue (#00629B) gradient with IEEE logo and society information
- **Circuit Pattern**: Subtle technical illustration showing chip architecture and circuit connections
- **Central Chip Illustration**: Abstract representation of a multi-core processor with pins and connections
- **Color Scheme**:
  - IEEE Blue (#00629B): Professional, authoritative
  - White: Clean, academic
  - Gray (#666, #999): Technical documentation

**Typography:**
- **Title**: Times New Roman serif, uppercase, bold - traditional IEEE style
- **Volume/Number**: Arial sans-serif in info box - clear hierarchy
- **Chinese Text**: SimHei font for bilingual accessibility

**Layout:**
- Classic IEEE Transactions format with header bar
- Centered title and volume information
- Information box for volume/issue/date
- Footer with ISSN and copyright information

**Symbolism:**
The circuit patterns and chip illustration represent the journal's focus on hardware engineering and silicon-based systems. The professional, understated design emphasizes technical rigor and academic authority.
