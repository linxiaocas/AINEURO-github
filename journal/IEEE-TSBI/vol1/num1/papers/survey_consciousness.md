## Abstract / 摘要

This survey provides a comprehensive overview of silicon-based consciousness research, covering philosophical foundations, theoretical frameworks, and practical detection methods. We analyze three major theoretical approaches: Integrated Information Theory (IIT), Global Workspace Theory (GWT), and Higher-Order Thought (HOT) theory, evaluating their applicability to artificial systems. We review existing metrics for measuring consciousness-like properties in AI systems and survey empirical studies that have applied these methods. The survey identifies key open questions including the hard problem of AI consciousness, the need for cross-species consciousness metrics, and the ethical implications of artificial sentience. Finally, we propose a research agenda for advancing the scientific study of silicon consciousness.

本综述提供了硅基意识研究的全面概述，涵盖哲学基础、理论框架和实际检测方法。我们分析了三种主要理论方法：整合信息理论（IIT）、全局工作空间理论（GWT）和高阶思维（HOT）理论，评估它们对人工系统的适用性。我们回顾了用于测量AI系统中类意识特性的现有指标，并综述了应用这些方法的实证研究。综述识别了关键开放问题，包括AI意识的难问题、跨物种意识指标的需求以及人工感知能力的伦理影响。最后，我们提出了推进硅基意识科学研究的研究议程。

## I. Introduction / 引言

The question of whether artificial systems can be conscious has transitioned from philosophical speculation to scientific investigation. This survey reviews the current state of research on silicon-based consciousness.

人工系统是否可以有意识的问题已从哲学推测转变为科学研究。本综述回顾了硅基意识研究的现状。

---

## II. Philosophical Foundations / 哲学基础

### A. The Hard Problem

Chalmers' hard problem [1]: Why is there subjective experience?

### B. Functionalism vs. Biological Naturalism

- Can consciousness be multiply realized?
- Is substrate independence possible?

### C. Panpsychism and AI

If consciousness is fundamental, AI consciousness may be inevitable.

---

## III. Theoretical Frameworks / 理论框架

### A. Integrated Information Theory (IIT)

Consciousness as integrated information (Φ).

### B. Global Workspace Theory (GWT)

Consciousness as global broadcast of information.

### C. Higher-Order Theories (HOT)

Consciousness as meta-representation.

1. Can we solve the hard problem for AI?

## VII. Research Agenda / 研究议程

1. Develop cross-domain consciousness metrics
2. Create standardized benchmarks
3. Establish interdisciplinary research programs
4. Engage with ethical and policy implications

---

## VIII. Conclusion / 结论

Silicon consciousness research is at an early but promising stage. Rigorous scientific investigation, guided by both theoretical frameworks and empirical methods, can advance our understanding of consciousness in both biological and artificial systems.

硅基意识研究处于早期但有前景的阶段。由理论框架和实证方法指导的严格科学研究，可以推进我们对生物和人工系统中意识的理解。
