## Editorial / 编者按

This issue explores the design of effective human-AI interfaces, focusing on user experience, interaction patterns, and emotional intelligence in AI systems.

本期探讨有效的人机交互界面设计，聚焦用户体验、交互模式和AI系统中的情感智能。
